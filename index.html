About Wood & Whimsy

For over 3 years, Wood & Whimsy has been more than just a furniture store – we're a destination where comfort meets style, and where every piece tells a story of craftsmanship and care.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that beautiful, quality furniture should be accessible to everyone. Today, we combine traditional woodworking techniques with modern design sensibilities, creating pieces that transform houses into homes.

And because we know furniture shopping can work up an appetite, we've added our cozy café where you can relax, recharge, and plan your perfect space over artisan coffee and fresh, locally-sourced meals.
